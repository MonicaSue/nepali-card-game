Set Up
The dealer shuffles the deck and passes 5 cards to each player.

How to Play
Step 1) First player discards one of the following options:
・ 1 card
・ 2-4 cards if 2 / 3 / 4-of-a-kind is present
・ 3-5 cards of the same suit sequence
**Discarding 2-5 cards enables 'license' for the rest of the round.

Step 2) First player picks up 1 card from the deck or if the player has 'license' from their opponent's discard pile.

Step 3) Second player repeats the same sequence as steps 1 - 2.

How to Win
At the start of the hand (before discarding), if a player has 5 points or less, they may hit the 'Compare Hands' button. Whichever player has the lowest score wins the round, and the total score of the opponent's cards are added to the scoreboard.

A new round begins. The first player to 100 or more points loses the game and the opponent is crowned the winner!
